Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7/16/26

Waypoint Ledger ("the app") is a private tool for tracking one family's paid time off, trips, and travel expenses. This policy explains what data the app stores and how it's handled.

What we collect

When you create the shared family account, we store the email address and password you provide (the password itself is never stored in plain text — it's handled securely by our authentication provider, Supabase). Once signed in, the app stores whatever you choose to enter: family member names, birthdates, gender (optional), job and PTO information, trip details, and expense records.

How it's used

All data is used solely to power the app's own features — displaying your family's PTO balances, trips, and packing lists back to you. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

Where it's stored

Data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database and authentication provider. Supabase's own privacy and security practices apply to data in transit and at rest. No other third-party services receive your data.

Your control over your data

You can delete individual family members, trips, or expenses at any time within the app. You can also permanently delete the entire account and all associated data using the "Delete account & all data" option in the app — this cannot be undone.

Children's information

This app is intended for private use by a single family to track information about their own household members, including minors, entered by a parent or guardian. It is not directed at children as independent users and does not knowingly collect data directly from children.
